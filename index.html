  1. HONOR Earbuds 3 Pro is not meant to be a medical equipment, the function is mainly used for adults to view the temperature and changes only, not for any medical purpose. The device is for presentation or demonstration purposes only and cannot be made available until the device has been brought into compliance with related regulations. AI temperature monitoring is not available in United Kingdom, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Czech, Finland and Romania.
  2. Data from the HONOR lab. 5.1 grams without ear tip refers to the lightest configuration, actual weight may vary depending on configuration. If you feel uncomfortable when wearing the earbuds, stop using them and consult your doctor.
  3. When active noise cancellation is turned on, it may weaken the perception of the surrounding sound and environment. Turn on this function in a safe environment.
  4. The charging data comes from the HONOR lab. After the earbuds are power off due to low power, put them into a fully charged charging case, and close the case cover, charge them for 5 minutes, and then take them out. Turn off active noise cancellation and connect them to the mobile phone to play AAC-encoded music at 50% volume until the earbuds are fully discharged. The actual usage time varies depending on conditions such as volume, sound source, environment, and usage habits.
  5. HONOR Earbuds 3 Pro is released on Feb.28, 2022. It is the world' s first coaxial dual-driver TWS earbuds with the rod shape which feature a dynamic driver of damper design with 0.6mm super amplitude and a ceramic treble driver. PZT refers to piezoelectric ceramics.
  6. The noise reduction depth data comes from the HONOR lab. 46dB is the maximum noise reduction depth of the product. The above data come from the HONOR Lab based on a large number of human in-ear testing. The actual effect varies depending on the user’s ear size, earmuffs, ear canal structure, wearing posture, chewing status, and movement.
  7. The call noise reduction performance comes from the HONOR lab, and the actual performance varies depending on the factors such as the surrounding environment and terminal.
  8. Data from the HONOR lab using an ear thermometer as control. About 80% chance of achieving ±0.3°C or less error. The actual error varies by person,environment,and other factors.
  9. The battery life data comes from the HONOR lab. The earbuds and the charging case are fully charged. The earbuds turn off active noise cancellation and awareness mode. The earbuds are connected to the mobile phone to play AAC-encoded music at 50% volume, until the earbuds are fully discharged and put into the charging case to charge to 100%. Continue to play music until the earbuds and charging case are fully discharged. The actual battery life varies, depending on conditions such as volume, sound source, environment, and usage habits.
  10. HONOR Earbuds 3 Pro is the world’s 1st TWS earbuds featuring 5C fast charging case with a maximum peak charging current up to 210mA and 42mAh battery capacity.
  11. Only if reverse charging is also available on the phone.
  12. Low-latency gaming is only available for a HONOR smart phone or tablet running Magic UI 4.0 above.
  13. The wireless audio reception is only available for HONOR Magic 4, HONOR Magic V, HONOR 50 running Magic UI 4.2 or above. Please contact HONOR customer service for supported models. More supported phone models are to be updated. Please refer to HONOR official information.
  14. The waterproof for daily use applies only to the earbuds, not the charging case. The earbuds are not hot water resistant, and the splash& water-resistant function cannot work forever. These functions may perform not well due to normal wear.
